Privacy & Terms

Last updated: June 2026

Information we collect

When you place an order or send a message through this site, we collect:

  • Your name and phone number
  • Your preferred pickup date and time
  • Any message or question you send us

This information is used only to process your order, arrange pickup, and answer your questions. We don't sell or share your information with third parties.

How we contact you

We use the phone number and any contact details you provide to confirm your order via Messenger. We may also message you to arrange pickup logistics or answer questions about your build.

Orders & pickup

Placing an order on this site reserves the build for you, but does not finalise the sale. Full payment and handover happen in person at pickup. Pickup times are Monday–Friday after 5:00pm, and Saturday–Sunday anytime (8am–8pm).

If you need to cancel or change a reserved order, please message us on Messenger as soon as possible.

Warranty

Each build comes with a warranty period stated on the product listing (typically 3–12 months), covering hardware faults from normal use. Physical damage, liquid damage, or unauthorised modifications are not covered.

Data storage

Order and contact information is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider. We retain this information only as long as needed to fulfil orders and provide support.

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy or how your information is used, message us via our Contact page.
